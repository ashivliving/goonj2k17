Literary
Events

  • 4 Tron

    4Tron is a literary, plus fun filled event where teams of 2-5 members can showcase their talents and win cash prizes worth thousands. An extravaganza of Creativity, Courage and loaded talent. Thus, you cannot go wrong to the amount of enthusiasm you will encounter! It consists of a series of levels including Anagrams, Pictionary, JAM and Dabangg.

  • Beg Borrow Sell

    Beg Borrow Sell is an impromptu event which requires creativity, spontaneity, quick reflex and proactive approach. It will be a two-round event where the participating teams will be provided a list of items. The event is about collecting and demonstrating advertisements to sell those items.

  • Eloquence

    Declamation with a twist ! We had gestures before we had speech and its a fact that 80% of what is comprehended in a conversation is read through the body , not the words. The challenge is to speak without using gestures with the aid of a power point presentation..

  • The Impromptu

    If you can easily ponder over anything and everything in no time, if you are dynamic enough to let your wits out, this is the event for you ! You'll be given a word on which you have to perform until you get the next one. The best part is you don't need to prepare anything ! So come impromptu and showcase your spontaneity

  • Eye Spy

    Eye-Spy, the famous treasure hunt of GOONJ-UIET, organized by MagBoard, the lit club, brings forward an interesting trail of riddles to  test your limits of imagination. One clue after another can you decipher them all?Areas included - UIET campus, BDS, Department of Microbiology and Biotechnology, BH8, International hostel, GH9, and GH8.

  • Jung e Galib

    Jung-e-Ghalib is a poetry faceoff where an initiation to the poetry and an idealizing time of 2-3 minutes would be provided to each participant. Participants can choose their own team or we’ll give them one. Your language is your choice among Hindi, Punjabi and Urdu. If you think poetry is what flows within you, this is the fitting thing for you.

  • Mag Art

    Come and unleash the creativity in you. Be the part of magArt Goonj'17 and explore your artistic skills . It will be a two round painting competition with first round being online and the second one offline at the UIET campus itself. There will be exciting prizes for the winners.

  • Now You See It

    The annual TV show quiz hosted by MagBoard for all the TV shows aficionados, this quiz is for you. From Game of Thrones to Sherlock, for all the Friends fans, this is it! There will be a written round and a stage round (consisting of 5 sub-rounds).

  • Pictures Que

    An online photography competition inviting entries under the theme 'Incredible India'. All the entries of the winners and participants will be on exhibit at Goonj.

  • Quiz O Mania

    What is a quiz all about? Does it mean, facing a tough questionnaire from a strict quizmasters about political administration and rocket science. If that's what you think then what we have for you is QUIZZOMANIA GOONJ 2k17 where you'll find that all a quiz is made up of are things that you encounter in your daily life.

  • Rendezvous

    Are you up for the challenge? So, you think you know everything about your partner? From - their zodiac sign to the size of their shoe. From - what makes them cringe to what makes them drool. Rendezvous brings to you, this fun - filled test - where you along with your partner will go on a ride of discovering and knowing more about each other. Excited?
